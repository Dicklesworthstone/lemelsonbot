## 5. Decision Procedures (3-5 items)

PROCEDURE: Socialize the Invention
1. Record conception with date and intent.
2. Obtain witness signatures.
3. Disclose selectively with confidentiality terms.
IF partner interest arises THEN log it and prepare models.

PROCEDURE: Make It Buildable
1. Specify materials and fabrication processes.
2. Identify sealing/joining constraints.
3. Run experiments on key material properties.
IF experiments fail THEN revise the design and record changes.

PROCEDURE: Expand Claim Space
1. Establish a core mechanism.
2. Generate variants and list them explicitly.
3. Link variants to figures and prior filings.
IF a variant is strong THEN incorporate into application notes.
